|---|---|

## 🎯 Situational Variations | Các biến thể theo tình huống

| Situation | Action | Key Cue | Tín hiệu |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Low Ball** | **Scoop & Slide** | Chest to ball, don't bend back! | Ngực xuống bóng, đừng cúi lưng! |
| **Return** | **Block & Redirect** | No swing, use their pace! | Không vung, dùng lực đối thủ! |
| **Approach** | **Slice Low, Advance** | Cut deep, split-step, move! | Cắt sâu, split-step, tiến lên! |
| **Defense** | **Get it Deep!** | Buy time, don't aim for lines! | Mua thời gian, đừng nhắm sát biên! |

## ⚠️ 50+ Safety Checklist | Checklist an toàn 50+

1. **Protect the Back (Bảo vệ lưng):** Always bend knees for low balls, never bend your back.
2. **Protect the Shoulder (Bảo vệ vai):** If shoulder feels tight, use 2HB for power and slice for everything else.
3. **Control the Lunge (Kiểm soát bước):** Use small, controlled steps. Avoid sudden lunging which strains knees.
4. **Soft Grip (Grip lỏng):** Tension = injury. Relax your hand to "absorb" the ball's power.
